Plastic Bottle Collection


SUMMARY

Join Our Plastic Bottle Collection Drive and Make a Difference!

DETAILED DESCRIPTION

Are you passionate about protecting the environment and want to be part of a meaningful initiative? Look no further! We are excited to announce our upcoming Plastic Bottle Collection Drive, and we are calling out to all enthusiastic volunteers like you to join us in this noble cause.

Details:

Date: Daily til 31st December 2023 Time: 11 am - 5 pm

To do:

Collect plastic bottles (drinking bottles, detergent bottles) from their neighbourhood/working space. Type of plastic: Type 1, 2 & 5 (refer to the picture below) Ensure that the collected plastic is sorted into its respective types. Sent to PSPK ECOHUB at Selayang.



How do I identify the type of plastic?

The easiest way to identify plastic is by locating three arrows forming a triangle with a specific number in the middle that's located at the base of the plastic. These international codes offer clarity to plastic identification based on chemical makeup and the grading for recycling.

Plastic pollution has become a pressing global issue, and we believe that every small step towards reducing it can create a huge impact. Our Plastic Bottle Collection Drive aims to raise awareness about the importance of recycling and properly disposing of plastic waste. By participating in this drive, you will help us in collecting plastic bottles that might otherwise end up in our oceans, harming marine life and the environment!

Don't miss this opportunity to be a part of something meaningful and contribute to a greener planet. Join our Plastic Bottle Collection Drive and be a catalyst for positive change. Together, we can make a significant impact on our environment.

Let's take the first step towards a plastic-free future. See you at the collection drive!

ABOUT PERTUBUHAN PERKHIDMATAN SOCIAL DAN PEMBANGUNAN KOMUNITI DAERAH GOMBAK, SELANGOR (PSPK)
Pspk stands for Perkhidmatan Sosial Pembangunan Komuniti. The Association of Social Services and Community Development of Gombak District, Selangor (Pspk) is a non-profitable welfare and charitable organization involved in community service since 4th July 2006. It is registered under the Registrar of Societies of Malaysia and is affiliated to the Selangor Council of Welfare & Social Development and tax-exempted organisation since 2009. PSPK is striving to be a community-based organisation which strives for sustainability.

PSPK is the official partner of International Islamic University Malaysia and University of Kuala Lumpur Foundation and also with All Party Parliamentary Group of Malaysia on Sustainable Development Goals (APPGM-SDG), and United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR).

This year PSPK is embarking a significant milestone, our 16th anniversary with slogan “Care for Community, Women in Sustainability”. PSPK’s vision is to empower women and children, living out their dreams with dignity & hope, thereby strengthening families and transforming communities. Our mission is to facilitate development of under privileged women and children irrespective of their creed and race, through life skill training and a life changing community enhancement program with the five guiding principles; Governed by Love, Stewardship, Effectiveness, People Centric Approach and Innovativeness.

PSPK serves the community in need by being a support structure that empowers the community to improve their livelihood aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDG). PSPK’s focus area are Community Development, Children Education, Women Empowerment, Economic and Environment Restoration. PSPK empower the marginalized women and youth living in urban poverty by providing the support structure to uplift their lives.

PSPK have been empowered the B40 women and youths from the community in to social entrepreneurship. The empowered community took the ownership of the social enterprises – a sustainability model promotes sustained, inclusive and sustainable economic growth, productive employment and decent work for all together with the responsibility to engage the people for community’s betterment. The holistic initiative of PSPK has progressively impacted many women and youths from the urban poor community. PSPK believes with a good partnership and high commitment, we can transform in the community significantly in a greater way.
