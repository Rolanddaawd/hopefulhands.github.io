Animal Welfare

The manpower provided by kind-hearted volunteers keeps the shelter running and helps us spread the
word about Second Chance Animal Society.

DETAILED DESCRIPTION

We welcome volunteers (especially groups) who are willing to lend a hand to help make the shelter a more comfortable home for our community of doggies. This simple ‘gotong-royong’ exercise is a great team bonding activity for students and co-workers. Time: 11.00am-3.00pm Tasks: Paint Away + What a Dirty Job! + It’s a Dog’s Day! Participation fee: any amount of donation is welcomed!

Paint Away

Time: 11.00am-3.00pm Duration: 4-hours Task: Paint Kennels What to bring: Paint brush, paint (we’ll provide you with the paint code)

What a Dirty Job!

Time: 1.00am-3.00pm Duration: 4-hours Task: Clean kennels, shelter compound, clear drains, general overall cleanliness.

What to bring: Just yourself!
It’s a Dog’s Day!

Time: 11.00am-1.00pm or from 1.00am-3.00pm Duration: 2-hours Task: Shampoo and give our dogs a scrub and apply tick-wash What to bring: Just yourself!

Make sure to

Bring your own drinking water and lunch! What to wear: Old shoes and clothes that you won’t mind getting wet and dirty, towels, a clean change of clothes

Ready to lend a paw? Join today to book your commitment a week in advance to volunteer at the shelter. Thank you in advance!!
