About Us



We connect INTI students with impactful NGO projects and events, empowering them to create positive change through community engagement and skill development. Join us in making a difference today. Our platform is designed to connect INTI students with meaningful NGO projects and events, providing a unique opportunity to make a difference in the community while gaining valuable experience and insights.

Conncting for Care

We believe that being part of something bigger than yourself is a fulfilling and enriching experience. B Connect with like-minded individuals who share your passion for social change. Our platform provides a space for INTI students to network with other students, NGOs, and professionals in the field, creating a strong support system for your social impact journey. Our mission is simple yet powerful: To bridge the gap between INTI students and NGOs, fostering a culture of social responsibility, and empowering students to contribute to causes they are passionate about. We're on a mission to create a platform that not only connects you to impactful projects but also nurtures your social conscience and encourages active involvement in your community.
